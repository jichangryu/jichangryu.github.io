Familiarity breeds contempt. <Aesop, The fox and the lion>
- 너무 익숙하면 무시하게 되는 법이다.
- 지나치게 친하면 예의가 사라진다
- If you know a person or situation very well, you can easily lose respectfor that person or become careless in that situation.
- 유사표현: Familiarity with evil breeds not contempt but acceptance. 악에 익숙해지면 미워하기보다 받아들이게 된다.
